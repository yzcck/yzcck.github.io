About Me (CV)

I received the B.S.degree in computer science from the Hangzhou Institute of Commerce in 1991, the M.S. degree in computer science from the Southeast University in 2000. I then received my Ph.D.degree in pattern recognition and intelligence system at the Nanjing University of Science and Technology (NUST) in 2004.

From 2005 to 2007, I was a postdoctoral researcher at the Nanjing University of Science and Technology (NUST). From 2009 to 2010, he was a visiting professor at the Robotics Institute in the Carnegie Mellon University. I am a professor in Yangzhou University since 2011.

My current research interests mainly focus on pattern recognition, machine learning, deep learning and its applications on computer vision and face recognition. I have published more than 80 scientific papers in pattern recognition, computer vision, deep learning and the related areas.
